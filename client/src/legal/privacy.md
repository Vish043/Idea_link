# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## 1. Information We Collect

IdeaConnect collects the following information:

- **Account Information:** Name, email address, password (hashed), role, skills, interests, bio, and avatar
- **Idea Information:** Ideas you post, including titles, descriptions, tags, and required skills
- **Activity Data:** Messages, collaboration requests, task assignments, and NDA agreements
- **Technical Data:** IP addresses, user agents (for NDA agreements), and connection logs

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain the platform services
- Enable communication between users
- Facilitate collaboration and team formation
- Store and display your profile and ideas
- Track NDA agreements and collaboration requests
- Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

## 3. Information Sharing

- **With Other Users:** Your profile information, ideas, and messages are visible to other users as part of the platform's functionality.
- **Public Information:** Ideas marked as "public" may be visible to all users. Ideas with "protected details" require NDA agreement before full access.
- **No Third-Party Sharing:** We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

## 4. Data Storage

- Your data is stored in our database (MongoDB).
- We use industry-standard security practices, but no system is 100% secure.
- You are responsible for keeping your account credentials secure.

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information through your profile settings
- Delete your account (contact us for account deletion)
- Request a copy of your data

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

- We use session-based authentication (JWT tokens) for login.
- We may use cookies for session management and security.
- We do not use third-party tracking or advertising cookies.

## 7. Data Retention

- We retain your data as long as your account is active.
- Deleted accounts may have their data removed, but some information may remain in backups or logs for a limited time.
- Messages and collaboration history are retained as part of the platform's functionality.

## 8. Security

- We use password hashing (bcrypt) to protect your credentials.
- We use HTTPS for secure data transmission.
- However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 9. Children's Privacy

IdeaConnect is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy at any time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

## 11. Contact

For questions about privacy or data handling, please contact us through the platform.
